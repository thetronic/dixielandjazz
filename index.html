The Eastside Rhythm Kings are a lively jazz band based in Oxford, England, known for their infectious rhythms and passion for traditional jazz. Formed by a group of accomplished musicians, the band brings the sounds of old-school Dixieland to life. They've become a popular fixture on the Oxford music scene, captivating audiences with their energetic performances at the Gladiator Club. Whether you're a seasoned jazz fan or just looking for a fun night out, the Eastside Rhythm Kings are sure to get your feet tapping and leave you with a smile.
